How to use it

This app works when your Mac and Tesla browser can reach each other on the same WiFi local network.

Option 1: USB iPhone tethering

This is the easiest and recommended option.

  1. Turn on Personal Hotspot under Settings on your iPhone
  2. Connect the iPhone to your Mac with a USB cable
  3. Join the iPhone WiFi network from the Tesla car
  4. Launch ExtraDisplay app on your Mac, click "Start"
  5. Visit the address from the Tesla web browser and click "Connect"

Option 2: Portable router

This method requires some knowledge with router settings.

  1. Turn on Personal Hotspot under Settings on your iPhone
  2. Connect the iPhone to your Mac with a USB cable
  3. Join the iPhone WiFi network from the Tesla car
  4. Launch ExtraDisplay app on your Mac, click "Start"
